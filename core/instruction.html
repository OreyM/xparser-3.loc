Instruction for XboxParser

1. php.ini

memory_limit = 2048M

extension = php_curl.dll

2. MySQL

Add table to your DataBase, from "rezerv" folder.

best_db.sql

discount_db.sql

game_db.sql

or_parcing.sql

Settings

In "core/config.php" add data to variables:

define(HOST, 'localhost or IP for your DataBase connection'); define(DATABASE, 'database name'); define(USERNAME, 'username'); define(PASSWORD, 'password');
